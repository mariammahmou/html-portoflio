The Best Movies According to Mariam

My top 3 favorite movies.

3 Idiots

This movie inspires me a lot. It talks about friendship, education, and following your passion instead of pressure.

The Pursuit of Happyness

A very motivating movie about never giving up, even when life is really hard. It always reminds me to keep trying.

Zootopia

A fun animated movie with a deep message about believing in yourself and breaking stereotypes.
